About

NEW ADVENTURES was created for the formerly incarcerated individuals that need help getting their life together upon being released back into their communities. I started NEW ADVENTURES to help returning citizens from committing the same mistakes again and landing back into prison. I have discovered through my own experience that there are numerous programs to help us reaclamate back into our communities, but are limited to what they have to offer. If you are in need of help and are really looking for a change and independence I suggest you give NEW ADVENTURES a try.

Furniture

No money to buy furniture for you new place? No problem! Here at NEW ADVENTURES we have the tools to get you what you need.

Refurbrished furniture for your home! Now isn't this awesome!!! So, you would ask yourself whats the catch rite? Free furniture! What!? Well, the hope is that you stay out of trouble. So in our eyes it isn't free. We grab all your information and set you up at one of our stores nearest you and there are terms to the deal of course. This is our companys' vision if you get the help you need once your out of prison it'll be a big asset in keeping you out of trouble. Obviously this is a one time deal funds are limited per person. We do have a group of individuals that have helped us on our journey and have made donations to our idea in hopes that we can make our community a better place! We have a store that you are able to come pick your choice of furniture from to add to your lovely new home. The minute you get yourself in trouble again obviously everything gets taken away for the next person in need and of course and you lose the chance to have this opportunity again. Thats the catch! We help you get you back on track and your job is to stay on track! That is the same with everything else we have to offer.

Cars

Anything you need to achieve from getting your license to registering and insuring your vehicle or even help with grants to help assist with buying your first vehicle. NEW ADVENTURES is where we can make this all happen for you if you are interested.

We all know that once we are released we need a means of transportation. Our vision is independence! The goal for us here at NEW ADVENTURES is to help you on your journey in getting a license, vehicle, registration and insurance if you do not have any of it or even if your missing one. We are just here to help you get it up and running. The rest is up to you. We have grants for everything that was mentioned above, but the funds are limited due to donations. If this is something you were to be interested in. Then make sure you click down below on how to get ahold of us and leave us your email and message with what help that you are interested in ,so that we can contact you as soon as possible with the information you need to get the process started.
